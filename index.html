About Agnis Labs

Agnis Labs is an intelligent systems company based in Hoffman Estates, Illinois, founded in 2025. We design AI-native, cloud-based platforms that automate workflows and compliance for regulated industries.

What we do today

We help technical and compliance teams replace fragmented tools and manual processes with cohesive, automated systems. By combining workflow automation, compliance logic, and analytics, we enable organizations to move faster while staying audit-ready.

How we build

Leveraging expertise in cloud-native architectures and distributed systems, we design for scalability, resilience, and security from day one—so platforms can evolve with your business and regulatory landscape.
